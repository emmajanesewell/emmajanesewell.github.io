Good Apples

Project Info:

Good Apples was an assignment completed for User Interface/User Experience design course at Seneca College. This was an entry level UX/UI course and aimed to teach design process from research to app creation. Good Apples is a meal-scheduling and recipe app.

Project Details:

  • School:Seneca College
  • Course:User Interface + User Experience Design
  • Technologies:Adobe XD, Invsion, Adobe Illustrator
  • Date:November 2019
