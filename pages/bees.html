bees matter animated video

I was tasked with making 30 second informative video. I chose to create an educational video on Canada's bee populations.

I wanted the video to highlight an environmental issue, with details relevant to living in Canada. The mood for the video was designed to be cheerful as the viewer follows the main character, the bee, around the screen. I made all illustration in Adobe Illustrator and I completed the animations using Adobe After Effects. Please watch 'Bees Matter' below on my YouTube channel.
