Project Brief

Create a user friendly app that helps people reduce food and plastics waste by helping them prepare their meals in advance and shop with curated grocery lists. This app will not only be functional for meal prep, cooking, and grocery lists but will have a social platform for community members to share recipe ideas and zero waste "life hacks" to encourage and support them on their journey.

Competitive Analysis

For the reason that the scope of this app spans two industries, meal prep and sustainability, competitors from both industries must be evaluated. As of 2020, there are few direct competitors in this niche market of meal prep and sustainable shopping while several exist within their own markets that we are able to evaluate. The most direct competitor is MealBoard, an app that supports meal prep planning, grocery lists, and pantry inventory to reduce food waste, but does not cross over into providing users with research into sustainable brands to seek out. To this end, the most popular app appears to be How Good, which allows users to scan barcodes and search products which are then ranked 0-3 on sustainability bench marks. In order to inform the standards for meal prep apps, two of the most popular apps were evaluated Yummly and Meal Lime. These apps aim to help people eat healthier through meal prep and are industry leaders within the meal prep sphere alone.
