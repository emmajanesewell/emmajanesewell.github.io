six bean

For my branding and graphics standards project, I chose to develop a coffee company.

The concept for 'six bean' was born out of wanting to create a coffee company that was uniquely from Toronto. The colours in the six bean logo are influenced by the colours of the Distillery District, a uniquely Toronto landmark. The name of 'six bean' is influenced by Toronto's popular nickname being the '6ix'. I wanted the brand mood to be urban and modern, and reflect the feeling of the city, while also being influenced by colours and trends popular in the coffee industry as well.
