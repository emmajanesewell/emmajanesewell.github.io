About

About

I'm Emma Sewell. I create clean and clever stand-out brand content for small businesses and entrepreneurs, so you can emerge and stand out in your industry.

It's my business to create for you a visual identity suite that is not only professional and tells your brand story, but helps you stand-out across social, web, and print applications. Each branding suite I create is 100% custom and designed intentionally with your brand values, personality, story, and goals in mind.

I was raised by a family of artists and tradespeople, so it is no wonder why I ended up in a creative trade. The entrepreneurial and artistic spirit is something I have always been surrounded by, and my passion is using my experience to help others bring their brands to life. Outside of my Creative Studio you can find me at the movies, at the gym, or camping in the summertime.

Services

Services

Visual Idenity Design

I specialize in visual identity design which includes everything your consumer sees when interacting with your brand. Logo, colour palette, fonts, graphics, patterns, and more for use on web, social, and print assets. I develop branding with a holistic view of your brand - your products, story, values, audience, and industry in order to create a visual identity that is wholly reflective of who you are and what you offer.

Social Media Design

Social Media Marketing is an important way to develop a two-way relationship with your customers and share important updates. However, are you unsure how to get your social feed to have that professional and cohesive look? I offer social media design services, where I design reusable social media graphics based on your branding and social marketing goals.

Print Design

While it may seem like most things are going digital, print design is still an important part of modern commerce. Extend your branding across your print assets for your business - business cards, flyers or posters, gift cards, loyalty cards, stickers, labels, and brochures are all print design services I offer.

Photography

Good photography can give your brand a human and relatable feeling. I have 6+ years of professional expereince in the photography industry, from shooting events, weddings, concerts, formal dinners, corporate headshots, and business locations. Photography done right makes a versatile asset to use across print, web, and social.
