Services

What I Do?

Branding + Graphic Design

Branding, which consists of setting clear guidelines of typography, colour, logo, and mood, creates a cohesive feel for your business that is easily recognizable to your customers. Employing various graphic design principles and strategies through branding, on your website, and on social will give your brand that trendy and noticable edge on your competition.

Web Development + Design

Every business in the 21st century needs a website. I excell in front-end (meaning 'visual', or what you see on a website) web development - creating custom designs, using templates, or employing a Wordpress site depending on your brands budget and specific needs.

UI/UX Optimization

Whats the use of having a website if your customers can't find any relevant information on it? We all know the feeling of a frustrating website, and it can make customers second guess or all together reject a brand's credibility. By using UI/UX optimization techniques I can build a new or improve an existing website to be user-friendly, mobile-friendly, and drive your brand goals.

Photography

Good photography can give your brand a human and relatable feeling. I have 6+ years of professional expereince in the photography industry, from shooting events, weddings, concerts, formal dinners, corporate headshots, and business locations. Photography done right makes veratile and informative assets for print, web, and social.

Social Media Managment

Social media is a great way to develop a two-way relationship with your customers. However, what makes it difficult is the constant upkeep and attention required to answer DMs, make frequent posts, and reply to comments. This is where I can step in - I will take the guess work out of what to post and when, and engage with your community daily.

SEO Marketing

One of the most recent skills added to my "digital strategy toolbox" is SEO (Search Engine Optimization) marketing. Ever wonder how Google decides what websites to diplay at the top of search results? In large part, this can be attributed to strong SEO strategy. Through careful planning, keyword selection, and web design I can help your brand land a high-ranking search result to get your business discovered.
